Research

I am currently working in the Cognitive Data Science Lab under Dr. Derek Powell where my research focuses on understanding and improving the reasoning processes of large language models (LLMs) by examining representations and taxonomic relationships. I leverage techniques like model editing to assess how interventions, such as injecting counterfactual data or manipulating logits, influence a model's performance and interpretability. More broadly, my research interests lie at the intersection of cognitive science, computational neuroscience, and artificial intelligence (AI).
